From "Impossible" to
Profession.

“She will go on and write her own book someday.”

These were the words of my history teacher during a PTM with my parents when I was in Class 10. I remember quietly smiling because back then, that felt impossible. I was choosing science. Writing was just something I loved, not something I believed I could pursue.

But as cliché as it sounds, what’s meant for you really does find you in ways you don’t expect.

Writing found me again in Class 11, when I started freelancing purely for the love of it without even knowing it could be commercialised. What began as passion slowly turned into a fun, fulfilling business.

I joined LinkedIn only a few months ago, and I’ve genuinely loved the journey. The doors that opened surprised me. Today, I work with TVM Creatives as a Content Writer & Strategist, handling international clients and helping them generate leads.

Looking back, I realise: you don’t always have to abandon one path to find another. Sometimes, passion quietly grows alongside you until one day, it becomes home.
