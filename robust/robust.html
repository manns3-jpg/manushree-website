The Process

How It Works

Simple 4-step process to convert your physical shares to digital format

1

Submit Documents

Share your physical certificates and required KYC documents with our team for verification.

2

Verification

We verify your documents and check the status of shares with respective company registrars.

3

Dematerialization

Your request is processed through NSDL/CDSL and shares are converted to electronic form.

4

Credit to Demat

Digital shares are credited to your demat account, ready for trading or holding.
